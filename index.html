Project Information

In this portfolio, it showcases 3 of my projects from the subjects : Filipino, Science, and Values. This projects have helped me to test my creativity by expressing my ideas and findings. .

In this science project, I experimented and observed how moisture and soil composition can affect the growth of a mung bean. The process required careful observation while the jar of mung beans are placed in a dark area that no sunlight can reach. It allows me to draw conclusions about the optimal conditions for plant development.

This Filipino project involves making a tagalog poem that talks about justice for people. This project helped me to appreciate the art of poetry and helped me deepen my appreciation for my own language, and the power of poetry to convey complex emotions and stories.

Lastly, My Values project. This showcases the process of making a habit formation. By creating a detailed list of habits that I want to do, I have been challenged for a 21-day journey of self improvement and discipline. This project helped me to be more consistent of the things I want to achieve and the importance of self reflection. .
